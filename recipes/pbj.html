Peanut Butter and Jelly

How to Make a Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

Peanut butter and jelly is a classic. Appropriate for any meal of the day, it's cheap, delicious, and easy. It only takes a few steps to make.

You can also put your own twist on it, by adding fruit or substituting various jams and nut butters.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Nut Butter or nut-free alternative
  • fruit-based jelly or jam

Steps

  1. Place your slices of bread on a plate side by side. Starting with the nut butter, use a butter knife to scoop a generous amount onto one slice of the bread. Spread around evenly.
  2. Using a different knife, do the same with the jelly.
  3. Add any fruit or toppings you desire. Get creative! You can add crushed nuts, sprinkles, or fruit slices.
  4. Put the two pieces of bread together and enjoy!
